#Law Research Assistant
An AI-powered legal research assistant leveraging Google's Gemini LLM. Designed to streamline legal information retrieval, document drafting, and comprehensive law research, inspired by Harvey.ai's domain expertise approach.

##Goals
Provide accurate, contextual legal insights using advanced LLM capabilities.

Automate summarization, citation, and drafting of legal documents.

Support diverse legal domains including litigation, regulatory compliance, and contracts.

Enable secure, privacy-conscious integration with legal workflows and document management systems.

Facilitate customization for specific jurisdictions, legal languages, and user preferences.

##Scope
Statutory and case law analysis with citation support.

Drafting assistance for briefs, contracts, and memos.

Integration-ready modules for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and multimodal reasoning.

Multi-language support reflecting global legal practice needs.

Modular prompt libraries and example workflows to accelerate adoption and extend functionality.

This repository aims to provide legal professionals, law firms, and AI developers a robust foundation for building scalable, reliable AI legal assistants based on the Gemini LLM.
